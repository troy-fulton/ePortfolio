Music

I have always loved listening to and playing classical music, especially big orchestral symphonies like Pines of Rome and Symphonic Metamorphosis. I have been playing trumpet since I was 9 years old, and I still can't put it down. In high school, I studied music theory and qualified twice in the Texas All-State Philharmonic Orchestra. I participate in the Texas A&M University Wind Symphony every semester to get my mind off of my most rigorous classes a few times a week, and I have loved getting to know the other instrumentalists and making incredible music.

Last May, the Wind Symphony was granted the priviledge of performing in Hawai'i (pictured above). I also participate in the Philharmonic Orchestra at Texas A&M offered to Wind Symphony players who want to volunteer. I have enjoyed playing symphonies like Nabucco Overture and Berber of Seville every semester I have been at Texas A&M.
